Auratraq Vehicle Tracking System combines the use of automatic vehicle location device installed in vehicles equipped with IoT SIM cards which sends data to Auratraq server which is hosted in Amazon cloud. With four or more satellites in view, the GPS tracker can determine car's location with three parameters - latitude, longitude and altitude. Once the car's location has been determined, the GPS tracker can calculate other information, such as speed, track, trip distance, distance to destination, idle time, parking time and more.

The collected data from devices are available to the registered users by logging in to auratraq.com web portal or via auratraq apps available for download from appstore and playstore. By logging in to auratraq, each user can see their fleets with relevant realtime and historical activities.

Auratraq have a user-interface which is very easy to understand and use with perfect graphics used to simplify the data retrieved from the GPS tracker. Auratraq platforms helps the users to generate several alerts and reminders with the most user-friendly platform very easily. Users can download reposts and relevant data and can schedule automatic reports to their emails.
